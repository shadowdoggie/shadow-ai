# Contributing to Shadow AI

First — thank you for your interest in Shadow AI. Please read this before opening
anything, because the contribution model here is intentionally unusual.

## Pull requests are not accepted

**Shadow AI is a personal project, built and directed solely by its author.** It is
shipped the way the author wants it to be.

- **Pull requests will not be reviewed or merged, and are closed automatically.**
- This is not personal and it's not about the quality of your code — it's a deliberate
  choice to keep the project's direction singular and consistent.
- Please **do not** spend your time preparing a PR for this repository; it will be closed
  unread.

## Issues are welcome (but no promises)

- You're welcome to open an **Issue** to report a bug or suggest an idea.
- Just know up front: **there is no guarantee it will be read, answered, or acted on.**
  Treat it as leaving a note, not as filing a ticket that gets worked.

## Want to change it yourself? Fork it.

Shadow AI is open source under **AGPL-3.0-or-later**. You are free to fork it and build
your own version — the AGPL just requires that your fork (including anything you run as a
network service) stays open under the same license. See [`LICENSE`](LICENSE).

## Platform

**Shadow AI is Windows-only, by design — now and going forward.** It is not a temporary
limitation, and there are no plans for macOS, Linux, or mobile builds. Issues or requests
asking for other platforms will not be actioned.
